### **1. Container Orchestration Tools**  
Manage deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.

- **Kubernetes** – Industry-standard container orchestration platform.  
- **Docker Swarm** – Native Docker clustering and orchestration.  
- **Apache Mesos** – Distributed systems kernel with container orchestration.  
- **Nomad** – Lightweight orchestrator by HashiCorp.  
- **OpenShift** – Kubernetes-based container platform by Red Hat.
